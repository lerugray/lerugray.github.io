## The Pete Burns thread — structural truth recursion

One Le Rug album is titled ***Party With Peter Burns*** — an explicit nod to Pete Burns of **Dead or Alive**, the synthpop frontman. Independently, **CatalogDNA** — Ray's own musical-fingerprinting tool — surfaced Pete Burns / Dead or Alive as the **canonical structural match** on Ray's own catalog. The framing from CatalogDNA's own docs: "Ray doesn't think of himself as synthpop-adjacent; the tool surfaced a structural similarity that made him reconsider his own work, and that defiance is the feature."

Put together, this is a two-layer self-awareness story:

- The **album title** says *"I know about Pete Burns."*
- The **fingerprint** says *"you sound more like him than you thought."*

The album is a self-aware homage. The tool reveals the influence runs deeper than a title — into chord vocabulary, scale choices, melodic shape. Ray's title was a nod; the tool says the nod is structural.

**This is arguably the strongest "living demo" story CatalogDNA has.** The CatalogDNA section on the site should link directly to *Party With Peter Burns* — a visitor reads the positioning, follows the link, hears the music, returns with the claim internalized. Treat this thread with care and confirm framing with Ray before shipping it.

## Prior bands

Before Le Rug, Ray was in two earlier bands he considers his most important pre-Le Rug work:

### Sweet Bulbs

Guitar / indie band. The most notable public credibility signal on Ray's music history: **Tyler, the Creator publicly praised Sweet Bulbs on Twitter multiple times**, verified via three distinct tweets:

1. *"i wish Sweet Bulbs made 'Junk' longer"* — https://twitter.com/tylerthecreator/status/165996940948025344
2. *"CHECK OUT 'SHAKE APPEAL' BY IGGY POP, AND 'KISSING CLOUDS' BY SWEET BULBS"* — https://twitter.com/tylerthecreator/status/591796727272747008
3. *"Sweet Bulbs Is What High School Was Suppose To Sound Like"* — https://twitter.com/tylerthecreator/status/27707055149

The *Junk* tweet is the one Ray referenced when he told me about this — the song he wrote and played guitar on that Tyler wished was longer. Handle with no embellishment: just cite the tweets and name the song.

### Butter The Children

Brooklyn guitar band. Dreampop / retro new wave. Members: **Inna Mkrtycheva** (vocals), **Jordyn Blakely** (drums), **J Boxer** (bass), **Ray Weiss** (guitar).

- **Bandcamp:** https://butterthechildren.bandcamp.com
- **Releases:**
  - Self-titled EP — released 2012-05-16, 4 tracks
  - *True Crime* — released 2014-08-29, 12 tracks
- **Context from the Bandcamp bio:** "ex-members of Sweet Bulbs & Night Manager." It's not clear from the bio whether **Ray** was specifically in Night Manager or whether that refers to other members — **do NOT claim Ray was in Night Manager** without his confirmation.
- **Press:** 2013 write-up in *New York Music Daily* framed them as "a catchy update on classic dreampop."
