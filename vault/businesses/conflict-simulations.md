## Model

- **Board wargames:** print-on-demand fulfillment. Small-batch, serious-hobbyist audience. Historical accuracy and game balance are non-negotiable — playtesting, developer refinement, community feedback.
- **Digital games:** sold directly on itch.io under the CSL brand. Built with AI-assisted coding — Ray is a wargame designer, not a programmer, and the digital side of the catalog exists because AI tools made it possible for him to ship code he couldn't write by hand. (See the bio in `user_ray_weiss.md`: "couldn't code for a decade, now uses AI tools.")

## Target audience

Historical wargame hobbyists, board-gamer history enthusiasts, and serious play-study audiences who will spot ahistorical glosses immediately. The digital side broadens reach to terminal-game / indie-sim players whose tastes overlap with wargame nerds.
