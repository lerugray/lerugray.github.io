# Conflict Simulations LLC — public positioning

_Source-of-truth for site copy. Ray's print-on-demand board wargame company. More context lives in the wargame design book project (`../../../passive-income-hub/`), which is Ray's own work._

## One-liner

A small print-on-demand board wargame publisher producing operational and strategic-scale historical wargames.

## Company

Ray owns and operates **Conflict Simulations LLC.** Public storefront: **[consimsltd.com/shop](https://consimsltd.com/shop)**.

## Ray's wargame credentials (public and safe)

- Designer of **~30 published wargames**.
- **CSR Award nominated.**
- Also published by **Multi-Man Publishing (MMP).**

## Titles (public)

Operational and strategic-scale historical wargames including: **1950 Korea, 1973 Arab-Israeli War, Alma, Koniggratz, Imperial Bayonets, DAMOS**, and others.

## Model

Print-on-demand fulfillment. Small-batch, serious-hobbyist audience. **Historical accuracy and game balance are non-negotiable** — playtesting, developer refinement, community feedback.

## Target audience

Historical wargame hobbyists, board-gamer history enthusiasts, serious play-study audiences who will spot ahistorical glosses immediately.
